Good candidates for a PEO

  • 5 to 150 employees
  • Owner or manager handling HR tasks themselves
  • Struggling to offer competitive benefits
  • Growing quickly or hiring across multiple states
  • High workers comp costs or recent claims
  • No dedicated HR staff on payroll
