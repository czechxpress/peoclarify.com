Book your free PEO assessment

A 20-minute conversation. We will tell you honestly whether a PEO makes sense, what it should cost, and which ones are worth talking to. No obligation, no pressure.

What to expect

  • 20 minutes, no fluff
  • We ask about your company size, industry, and current HR setup
  • We tell you honestly if a PEO is the right fit
  • If it is, we explain the cost range for a company your size
  • If it is not, we tell you that and point you in the right direction
  • Zero obligation to move forward

Good candidates for a PEO

  • 5 to 150 employees
  • Owner or manager handling HR tasks themselves
  • Struggling to offer competitive benefits
  • Growing quickly or hiring across multiple states
  • High workers comp costs or recent claims
  • No dedicated HR staff on payroll

No cost to you. Our advisory service is free to employers. We are compensated by the PEO you choose, which means we are motivated to find you the right fit, not the most expensive one.
